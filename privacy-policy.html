1. Information We Collect

Ali Tools Company is designed with your privacy as a top priority. Because all file processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript, we never receive, store, or have access to any files you process on our website.

We may collect minimal non-personal information through standard web analytics:

• Pages visited and time spent on each page
• Browser type and version
• Device type and operating system
• Approximate geographic location (country level only)
• Referring website or search term

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally. We use this data solely to improve our website and understand which tools are most popular.

2. How We Use Your Information

The anonymous analytics data we collect helps us:

• Improve and optimize our tools and user interface
• Identify and fix technical issues
• Understand user behavior patterns to enhance the experience
• Determine which tools to prioritize for new features

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not display personalized ads based on your browsing behavior.

3. Cookies

Ali Tools Company uses minimal cookies for essential functionality:

• Local Storage: We may use browser local storage to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode) if we implement it in the future. This data never leaves your browser.
• Analytics Cookies: We use privacy-focused analytics that do not track individual users or store personal data.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. However, please note that essential functionality may be affected. Most of our tools work perfectly without cookies.

4. Third Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services, all of which are privacy-compliant:

• Google Fonts: For typography. No personal data is shared.
• CDN Libraries: We load JavaScript libraries from CDN providers. These providers may see your IP address as part of standard web requests.
• Analytics Provider: Privacy-focused, anonymous analytics service.

We carefully vet all third-party services to ensure they respect user privacy and comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

5. Data Security

Because your files never leave your device, there is no risk of your data being intercepted, stored, or misused during file processing. The key security features of our platform include:

• Client-Side Processing: All file operations occur entirely within your browser using JavaScript. No files are uploaded to our servers.
• HTTPS Encryption: Our website is served over HTTPS, encrypting all communication between your browser and our server.
• No Data Storage: We do not maintain databases of user files, accounts, or personal information.
• No Tracking: We do not use fingerprinting, tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

Ali Tools Company is a general audience website and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will promptly remove such information from our records.

We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.
