What Is Grayscale Conversion?

Grayscale conversion is the process of transforming a color image into shades of gray, removing all color information while preserving the luminance or brightness structure. Each pixel's red, green, and blue values are combined using specific mathematical formulas to produce a single gray value ranging from black to white. Different conversion methods produce different visual results: luminosity weighting mimics human perception, averaging gives equal weight to all channels, and desaturation provides a balanced middle ground. Our free online grayscale converter processes images directly in your browser for instant, private results.
