What Is an Image Collage Maker?

An image collage maker combines multiple photos into a single composite image, typically arranged in a grid or custom layout. Collages are used to tell visual stories, compare images, create mood boards, and share multiple photos in a single post on social media. Our free online collage maker arranges your images in a clean grid layout with customizable columns, spacing, and background color. All processing happens directly in your browser for complete privacy and instant results.

Why Use Our Collage Maker?

Creating collages traditionally requires desktop software like Photoshop or complex online tools that upload your images to servers. Our tool simplifies the process: upload 2-6 images, choose your layout preferences, and download a perfectly stitched collage. The grid layout automatically resizes images to fit evenly, maintaining consistent spacing throughout. Whether you are creating before-and-after comparisons, photo grids for Instagram, or project mood boards, our collage maker delivers professional results in seconds.
