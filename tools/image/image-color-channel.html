What Is

Image Color Channel Manipulator is a tool that allows you to process digital images — making it larger or smaller by adjusting its width and height in pixels. Unlike cropping which removes parts of an image, processing scales the entire image proportionally or non-proportionally to fit specific requirements. Our free online Image Color Channel Manipulator processes all images directly in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API, meaning your files are never uploaded to any server. This makes it one of the fastest and most private image processing tools available online.
