What Is an Image Flipper and Rotator?

An image flipper and rotator is a tool that lets you change the orientation of a digital image by mirroring it (flipping) or turning it around a central axis (rotating). Flipping creates a mirror image either horizontally (left becomes right) or vertically (top becomes bottom). Rotating turns the image by a specified angle, typically 90, 180, or 270 degrees. Our free online tool processes all transformations directly in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API, ensuring your images remain private and processing is instantaneous.
