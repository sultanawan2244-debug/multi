What Is an Image Border Tool?

An image border tool lets you add a decorative or functional frame around digital images. Borders can be used to separate images from backgrounds, add visual polish, create consistent framing for galleries, or prepare images for printing with white margins. Our free online tool lets you customize border width, color, and optional rounded corners, all processed directly in your browser for complete privacy and instant results.

Why Use Our Border Tool?

Adding borders to images is a common requirement for social media graphics, product photography, digital scrapbooking, and portfolio presentations. Our tool makes it trivially easy with intuitive controls and instant preview. Choose a thin black border for a classic photo look, a white border for Instagram-style framing, or colorful borders for creative projects. Rounded corners add a modern touch that softens the image edges.

Key Features

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing. No uploads.
  • Adjustable Width: Border thickness from 1 to 200 pixels.

  • Rounded Corners: Optional corner radius for modern framing.

Use Cases

Social media managers add consistent borders to Instagram posts; photographers frame portfolio images; e-commerce sellers add white borders to product shots; graphic designers create visual separation in layouts; scrapbooking enthusiasts decorate digital photos; and anyone preparing images for print adds margins to prevent edge cropping.
