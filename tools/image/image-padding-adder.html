What Is Image Padding?

Image padding adds empty space around the edges of an image, increasing its overall dimensions without stretching or distorting the original content. This is useful for creating margins, fitting images into specific frame sizes, or adding space for text overlays.

Why Use Our Padding Tool?

Our tool gives you independent control over top, right, bottom, and left padding with a customizable background color. Perfect for preparing images for social media, print, or web layouts.
