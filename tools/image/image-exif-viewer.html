What Is EXIF Data?

EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) data is metadata embedded in digital photos by cameras and smartphones. It contains a wealth of information including camera make and model, exposure settings (shutter speed, aperture, ISO), date and time the photo was taken, GPS coordinates, focal length, flash usage, and even thumbnail previews. This data is invaluable for photographers analyzing their shots, law enforcement verifying image authenticity, and anyone who wants to know the technical details behind a photograph. Our free online EXIF viewer reads this metadata directly in your browser without uploading files anywhere.

Why Use Our EXIF Viewer?

Understanding EXIF data helps photographers improve their technique by reviewing which settings produced the best results. It helps verify image authenticity and origin.
