What Is Pixel Art?

Pixel art is a form of digital art where images are created at the pixel level, typically with a limited color palette and visible blocky pixels that evoke retro video games and early computer graphics. Our converter transforms regular photos into pixel art by reducing resolution and quantizing colors, creating that nostalgic 8-bit aesthetic.

Why Use Our Pixel Art Converter?

Pixel art is popular for game development, social media profiles, merchandise design, and creative projects. Our tool makes it easy to convert any image into pixel art with adjustable pixel size and color count.
