What Is an Image Watermark Tool?

An image watermark tool lets you overlay text, logos, or patterns onto digital images to assert ownership, prevent unauthorized use, or brand your visual content. Watermarks can be transparent or opaque, positioned anywhere on the image, and repeated across the surface for comprehensive protection. Our free online image watermark tool processes all images directly in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API, ensuring your original files and watermarked results never leave your device. This makes it one of the most private and secure watermarking solutions available.

Why Use Our Image Watermark Tool?

Online watermarking services often require uploading images to remote servers, which can expose your work to privacy risks and potential theft. Our tool eliminates these concerns entirely by processing everything locally in your browser. You have full control over the watermark text, font size, opacity, color, and positioning. The tiling option lets you covers the entire image with repeating watermarks for maximum protection. Whether you are a photographer protecting your portfolio, a designer branding your mockups, or a business owner securing product images, our watermark tool gives you professional results in seconds.
