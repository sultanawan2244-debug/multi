What Is Image to ASCII Art?

Image to ASCII art conversion transforms a digital image into a text-based representation using characters from the ASCII standard. Each character represents a specific brightness level or tone, with denser characters like @ and # representing darker areas and lighter characters like . and spaces representing lighter areas. When viewed in a monospace font, these characters form a recognizable image made entirely of text. Our free online converter processes images directly in your browser, offering multiple character sets and adjustable width for creative control.
