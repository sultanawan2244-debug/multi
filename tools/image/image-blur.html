What Is an Image Blur Effect?

An image blur effect softens or obscures details in a digital image by averaging pixel values with their neighbors. Different blur algorithms produce different visual results: box blur averages pixels in a rectangular neighborhood, creating a uniform softening; Gaussian blur uses a weighted average based on the Gaussian distribution, producing a smoother, more natural-looking blur; pixelate replaces groups of pixels with a single average color, creating a mosaic effect. Our free online image blur tool processes all effects directly in your browser, ensuring your images remain private while delivering professional-quality results.
