What Is Color Inversion?

Color inversion, also known as the negative effect, is a process that transforms each pixel's color to its opposite on the color wheel. For RGB images, each channel value is subtracted from 255: red becomes cyan, green becomes magenta, blue becomes yellow, and white becomes black. This creates a striking visual effect similar to photographic film negatives. Our free online color inverter processes images directly in your browser, giving you instant negative effects with no upload required.

Why Use Our Color Inverter?

Color inversion serves both creative and practical purposes. Artists use it to create surreal or dramatic effects. Designers invert images for dark mode compatibility. Accessibility professionals test color contrast by inverting images. Developers debug visual layouts with inverted colors. The effect is also useful for creating X-ray-like visuals and for certain types of image analysis where inverted colors reveal hidden details.
