What Is an Image Cropper?

An image cropper is a tool that lets you remove unwanted outer areas from a digital photo or graphic by selecting a rectangular region to keep. Unlike resizing which scales the entire image, cropping removes pixels from the edges to improve composition, change the aspect ratio, or focus attention on a specific subject. Our free online image cropper processes all images directly in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API, so your files never get uploaded to any server. This approach ensures both speed and privacy, making it one of the most secure image cropping tools available online.
