What Is Background Color Replacement?

Background color replacement is a technique that changes a specific color (typically a uniform background) to another color in a digital image. This is commonly used for product photography, passport photos, and graphic design where a clean, consistent background is needed. Our tool lets you replace any background color with a new one by selecting the target color and adjusting tolerance for how similar colors should be included. All processing happens in your browser for instant, private results.
