What Are Brightness and Contrast Adjustments?

Brightness and contrast are two fundamental image adjustment parameters. Brightness controls the overall lightness or darkness of an image, shifting all pixel values up or down uniformly. Contrast controls the difference between the lightest and darkest areas of an image, affecting the tonal range and perceived sharpness. When you increase contrast, light pixels become lighter and dark pixels become darker, making the image appear more vivid and defined. Our free online brightness and contrast adjuster processes images directly in your browser, offering precise control with intuitive sliders ranging from -100 to +100 for both parameters.
