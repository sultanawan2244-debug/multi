What Is Sepia Tone?

Sepia tone is a reddish-brown monochrome effect that mimics the look of vintage photographs from the 19th and early 20th centuries. Originally caused by the chemical process of early photographic prints, sepia has become a popular artistic effect for giving images a nostalgic, old-fashioned appearance.
