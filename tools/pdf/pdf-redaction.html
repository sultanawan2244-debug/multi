What Is a PDF Redaction Tool?

A PDF Redaction Tool permanently removes sensitive information from PDF documents by covering content with colored rectangles. Unlike simply deleting content, redaction ensures the underlying data cannot be recovered. This tool supports redaction by text search, full page redaction, or redacting all content.

Why Use Our PDF Redaction Tool?

Our tool runs entirely in your browser with no server uploads. Redact sensitive information like names, addresses, financial data, or classified content quickly and securely.
