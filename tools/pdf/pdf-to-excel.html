What Is PDF to Excel Converter?

PDF to Excel Converter is a free online tool that extracts text content from PDF documents and creates an Excel-compatible CSV file. Using pdf.js for text extraction, our tool processes PDFs entirely in your browser to generate a UTF-8 CSV (Comma-Separated Values) file that opens directly in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, Apple Numbers, and LibreOffice Calc. Each page is represented as a row with page number, text content, and word count columns.

Why Use Our PDF to Excel Converter?

Most online PDF to Excel converters require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser, you can convert PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
