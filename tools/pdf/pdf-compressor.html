What Is a PDF Compressor?

A PDF Compressor reduces the file size of PDF documents by removing unnecessary data, optimizing internal structures, and applying efficient encoding. Smaller PDF files are easier to email, upload, and store. Our free online PDF Compressor processes your file entirely in your browser, meaning your PDF is never uploaded to any server. This makes it one of the most private PDF compression tools available online.

Why Use Our PDF Compressor?

Most online PDF compressors require you to upload your document to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can compress PDFs instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
