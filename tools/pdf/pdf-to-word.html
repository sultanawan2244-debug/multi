What Is PDF to Word Converter?

PDF to Word Converter is a free online tool that extracts text from PDF documents and creates a Word-compatible document. Using pdf.js for text extraction, our tool processes PDFs entirely in your browser and generates a Word-compatible HTML file (.doc extension) that can be opened in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LibreOffice Writer, and other word processors. Note that this is a text extraction approach — complex PDF layouts may not be preserved in full fidelity.

Why Use Our PDF to Word Converter?

Most online PDF to Word converters require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser, you can convert PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
