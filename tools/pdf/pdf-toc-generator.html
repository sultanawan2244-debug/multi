What Is a PDF TOC Generator?

A PDF TOC (Table of Contents) Generator creates a structured table of contents page at the beginning of your PDF document. It adds entry pages listing section headings and their corresponding page numbers, making navigation easier for readers. This is essential for reports, eBooks, manuals, and long documents.
