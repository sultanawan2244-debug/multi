What Is a PDF Layer Viewer?

A PDF Layer Viewer displays the layer structure (Optional Content Groups - OCG) in PDF documents. Layers are used in CAD drawings, maps, and multi-language documents. pdf-lib has limited OCG support, so this tool focuses on page-level structure information.

Why Use Our PDF Layer Viewer?

Analyze your PDF's page structure quickly in your browser. Get page dimensions, rotation, and layout information without uploading to any server.
