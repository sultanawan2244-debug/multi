What Is PDF Watermarking?

PDF watermarking is the process of adding text or images over PDF pages to indicate ownership, confidentiality, or status. Our PDF Watermark tool lets you add customizable text watermarks to every page of your PDF documents. Unlike online services that require file uploads, all processing happens entirely in your browser using pdf-lib, ensuring your documents remain private and secure.

Why Use Our PDF Watermark Tool?

Most online PDF watermark tools require you to upload your documents to a server, creating privacy risks and slow upload times. Our tool eliminates these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can add watermarks to PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.

Key Features of Our PDF Watermark Tool

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing. No uploads, no servers, no data transfer.
  • Customizable Text: Enter any watermark text you want to appear on your pages.
  • Adjustable Size: Control font size from small to very large.
  • Opacity Control: Set transparency level from subtle to bold.
  • Position Options: Place watermark at center, corners, or edges.
  • Rotation: Tilt watermark text at any angle from -90° to 90°.
  • Color Picker: Choose any color for your watermark text.
  • No Limits: Process PDFs of any page count (within browser memory limits).
