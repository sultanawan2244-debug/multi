What Is PDF Word Counter?

PDF Word Counter is a free online tool that analyzes the text content of PDF documents to provide detailed word count statistics, character counts, page counts, and word frequency analysis. Using pdf.js for text extraction, our tool processes PDFs entirely in your browser to deliver comprehensive text analytics. The tool displays total words, total characters (with and without spaces), total pages, and a ranked list of the most frequently used words in the document.

Why Use Our PDF Word Counter?

Most online PDF word counters require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using pdf.js, you can analyze PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.

Key Features of Our PDF Word Counter

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing using pdf.js. No uploads, no servers, no data transfer.
  • Word Count: Accurate total word count from all pages of the PDF.
  • Character Count: Total characters including and excluding spaces.
  • Page Count: Number of pages in the PDF document.
  • Word Frequency: Top words ranked by frequency, with bar visualization.
  • Download Report: Export the full analysis as a text file.

Use Cases for PDF Word Counter

Our PDF Word Counter is perfect for a wide range of scenarios: students check word counts for essays and assignments; writers track their document length; editors analyze word usage patterns; content managers measure document scope; researchers perform basic text analysis; and professionals ensure documents meet word count requirements.
