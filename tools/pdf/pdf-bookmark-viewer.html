What Is a PDF Bookmark Viewer?

A PDF Bookmark Viewer lets you examine the page structure and layout of PDF documents. While true bookmark/outline extraction requires advanced PDF libraries, this tool provides a comprehensive view of all pages including their indices, dimensions, and rotation information. It helps you understand the document structure before editing or reorganizing pages.

Why Use Our PDF Bookmark Viewer?

Most online PDF bookmark viewers require uploading your files to a server. Our tool runs entirely in your browser using pdf-lib, keeping your documents private and secure. You get instant analysis without file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions.

Key Features of Our PDF Bookmark Viewer

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing. No uploads, no servers.
  • Page Listing: View all pages with their indices in the document.
  • Page Dimensions: See width and height of each page.
  • Rotation Info: Check page rotation settings.
  • Total Count: Quick overview of total pages in the document.

Use Cases for PDF Bookmark Viewer

Our PDF Bookmark Viewer is useful for document reviewers who need to understand document structure, editors preparing PDFs for reorganization, and quality assurance teams verifying document integrity. It helps identify how many pages a document has and their properties before processing.
