What Is PDF Bates Numbering?

Bates numbering (also known as Bates stamping) is a method of sequentially numbering pages in legal, medical, and business documents for unique identification. Each page receives a unique number, typically with a prefix and zero-padded digits (e.g., "DOC-00001"). Our tool processes everything in your browser using pdf-lib.

Why Use Our PDF Bates Numbering Tool?

Traditional Bates stamping requires specialized software. Our free online tool provides the same functionality directly in your browser. No uploads, no server processing — your documents stay private and secure on your device.
