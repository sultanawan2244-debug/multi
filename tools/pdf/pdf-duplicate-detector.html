What Is a PDF Duplicate Detector?

A PDF Duplicate Detector finds pages with identical dimensions and properties in a PDF document. Duplicate pages often occur when documents are assembled from multiple sources or when pages are accidentally inserted multiple times.

Why Use Our PDF Duplicate Detector?

Identify duplicate pages in your PDF quickly without uploading to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Key Features of Our PDF Duplicate Detector

  • Dimension Comparison: Detects pages with identical dimensions.
  • Duplicate Groups: Shows groups of duplicate pages with indices.
  • Report Export: Download results as JSON.
  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing.
