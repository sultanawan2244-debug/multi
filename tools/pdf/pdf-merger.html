What Is a PDF Merger?

A PDF merger is a tool that combines multiple PDF files into a single PDF document. Instead of juggling several separate files, you can merge them into one organized document. Our PDF merger runs entirely in your browser using the pdf-lib library, meaning your files never leave your device. This makes it one of the fastest, most private, and most secure PDF merging tools available online with zero file size limits.

Why Use Our PDF Merger?

Most online PDF mergers require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can merge PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees. Whether you're merging 2 PDFs or 20, the process is instant and completely free.
