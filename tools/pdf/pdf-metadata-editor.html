What Is a PDF Metadata Editor?

A PDF Metadata Editor lets you view and modify the metadata embedded in a PDF document. Metadata includes information like the document title, author name, subject, and keywords — data that helps with document organization and searchability. Our free online PDF Metadata Editor processes your file entirely in your browser, meaning your PDF is never uploaded to any server.

Why Use Our PDF Metadata Editor?

Most online PDF metadata editors require you to upload your document to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can edit PDF metadata instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
