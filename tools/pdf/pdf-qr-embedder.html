What Is a PDF QR Embedder?

A PDF QR Embedder adds QR codes to PDF documents. QR codes can contain URLs, text, or contact information. This tool generates a QR code from your input and places it on any page of your PDF at your chosen position and size.

Key Features

  • Custom Content: Any URL or text can be encoded.
  • Position Control: Place QR in any corner of any page.
  • Adjustable Size: Resize the QR code from 50 to 200 pixels.
  • Live Preview: See the QR code before embedding.
