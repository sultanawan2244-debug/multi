What Is PDF Cropping?

PDF cropping lets you trim unwanted margins, whitespace, or edges from PDF pages. Unlike resizing, cropping removes portions of the page to focus on the content you need. Our PDF Cropper tool uses pdf-lib's built-in crop box functionality to precisely trim margins from every page. All processing happens in your browser.

Why Use Our PDF Cropper?

Online PDF cropping tools typically require uploading documents to servers. Our tool processes everything in your browser, keeping your documents secure. No data caps, no file size limits, and completely free to use.

Key Features

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side. No uploads.
  • Independent Margins: Set different values for top, bottom, left, and right.
  • Precise Points: Enter exact margin values in PDF points (1/72 inch).
  • Preview Dimensions: See how cropping affects page size.
