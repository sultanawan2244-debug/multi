What Is a PDF Version Converter?

A PDF Version Converter lets you change the PDF specification version of a document. Different PDF versions support different features — for example, PDF 1.3 is compatible with older Acrobat 4 readers, while PDF 1.7 includes modern features like advanced encryption and 3D content. Our free online PDF Version Converter processes your file entirely in your browser, meaning your PDF is never uploaded to any server.

Why Use Our PDF Version Converter?

Most online PDF version converters require you to upload your document to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions.
