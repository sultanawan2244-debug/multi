What Is PDF to EPUB Converter?

PDF to EPUB Converter is a free online tool that extracts text from PDF documents and creates an EPUB eBook file. Using pdf.js for text extraction and JSZip for EPUB packaging, our tool processes PDFs entirely in your browser to create a minimal standards-compatible EPUB file. Each page is converted to an XHTML chapter, and the EPUB structure is assembled with proper metadata, table of contents, and container format.

Why Use Our PDF to EPUB Converter?

Most online PDF to EPUB converters require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser, you can convert PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
