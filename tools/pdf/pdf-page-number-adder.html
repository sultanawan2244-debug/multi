What Is PDF Page Numbering?

PDF page numbering adds sequential numbers to each page of a PDF document, making it easier to navigate, reference, and organize multi-page documents. Our PDF Page Number Adder lets you add customizable page numbers to any PDF. Unlike online services that require file uploads, all processing happens entirely in your browser using pdf-lib, ensuring your documents remain private and secure.

Why Use Our PDF Page Number Adder?

Most online PDF page numbering tools require you to upload your documents to a server, creating privacy risks and slow upload times. Our tool eliminates these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can add page numbers to PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.

Key Features of Our PDF Page Number Adder

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing. No uploads, no servers, no data transfer.
  • Page X of Y: Optionally show total page count alongside current page number.
  • Custom Starting Number: Begin numbering from any number you choose.
  • Flexible Position: Place numbers at any corner or center position.
  • Adjustable Font Size: Match the numbering style to your document.
