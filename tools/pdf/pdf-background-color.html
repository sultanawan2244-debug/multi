What Is PDF Background Color?

PDF background color changes the background of every page in your PDF by drawing a full-page colored rectangle behind all existing content. This is useful for creating colored documents, preparing files for specific use cases, or simply changing the look of your PDF. All processing happens in your browser using pdf-lib.

Why Use Our PDF Background Color Tool?

Most online PDF tools require uploading documents to servers. Our tool processes everything in your browser, keeping your documents private and secure. No uploads, no data caps, completely free.
