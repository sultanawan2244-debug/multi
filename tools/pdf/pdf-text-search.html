What Is PDF Text Search?

PDF Text Search is a free online tool that allows you to search for words and phrases within PDF documents. Using pdf.js for text extraction, our tool processes PDFs entirely in your browser to find all occurrences of your search term. Results are displayed with page numbers and surrounding context, making it easy to locate exactly where information appears in your document. Whether you need to find a specific term in a report, locate data in a research paper, or search through legal documents, PDF Text Search handles it all locally and securely.

Why Use Our PDF Text Search?

Most online PDF search tools require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using pdf.js, you can search through PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.

Key Features of Our PDF Text Search

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing using pdf.js. No uploads, no servers, no data transfer.
  • Multi-Page Search: Searches through every page of the PDF document automatically.
  • Context Display: Shows surrounding text for each match to provide context.
  • Page Number Reference: Each result includes the page number where it was found.
  • Match Count: Displays total number of matches found in the document.
  • Case-Insensitive: Search matches regardless of case for comprehensive results.

Use Cases for PDF Text Search

Our PDF Text Search is perfect for a wide range of scenarios: researchers search for specific terms in academic papers; lawyers find relevant clauses in legal documents; students locate information in textbooks; editors check for consistency across documents; data analysts find specific data points in reports; and professionals quickly navigate through lengthy PDF documents to find the information they need.
