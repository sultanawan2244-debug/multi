What Is a PDF Splitter?

A PDF splitter is a tool that extracts specific pages from a PDF document into a new PDF file. Instead of having one large document, you can split it into smaller, more manageable files. Our PDF splitter runs entirely in your browser using the pdf-lib library, meaning your files never leave your device. This makes it one of the fastest, most private, and most secure PDF splitting tools available online with zero file size limits.

Why Use Our PDF Splitter?

Most online PDF splitters require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can split PDFs of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
