What Are PDF Headers and Footers?

Headers and footers are repeating text elements that appear at the top (header) and bottom (footer) of every page in a document. They typically display document titles, chapter names, dates, or confidentiality notices. Our PDF Header Footer tool lets you add custom text to both the top and bottom of each page in your PDF. All processing happens in your browser using pdf-lib.

Why Use Our PDF Header Footer Tool?

Most online tools require uploading documents to servers, creating privacy concerns. Our tool processes everything in your browser, keeping your documents secure. No data caps, no file size limits, and completely free.
