What Is a PDF Page Extractor?

A PDF Page Extractor is a tool that lets you select and save specific pages from a PDF document as a new PDF file. Whether you need to extract a few important pages from a large report, save a single chapter from an ebook, or pull out specific sections from a contract, a page extractor makes it easy. Our free online PDF Page Extractor processes your file entirely in your browser, meaning your PDF is never uploaded to any server.

Why Use Our PDF Page Extractor?

Most online PDF page extractors require you to upload your document to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can extract PDF pages instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
