What Is a PDF Page Rotator?

A PDF Page Rotator is a tool that lets you change the orientation of pages in a PDF document. Whether you need to rotate a scanned document that was uploaded sideways, fix a mix of portrait and landscape pages, or simply adjust the viewing angle, a page rotator makes it easy. Our free online PDF Page Rotator processes your file entirely in your browser, meaning your PDF is never uploaded to any server. This makes it one of the fastest and most private PDF rotation tools available online.

Why Use Our PDF Page Rotator?

Most online PDF rotators require you to upload your document to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can rotate PDF pages instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees. Rotate a single page or every page in a 500-page document in a fraction of a second.
