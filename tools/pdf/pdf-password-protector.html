What Is a PDF Password Protector?

A PDF Password Protector is a tool that adds password-based encryption to your PDF documents, ensuring only authorized people can open and view the content. Our free online PDF Password Protector processes your file entirely in your browser, meaning your PDF and password are never uploaded to any server. This makes it one of the most secure PDF encryption tools available online.

Why Use Our PDF Password Protector?

Most online PDF password tools require you to upload your document to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which means your sensitive document and password pass through an external server. Our tool eliminates this security risk entirely. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, your PDF and password never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Key Features of Our PDF Password Protector

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing. No uploads, no servers, no data transfer.
  • Strong Encryption: Uses PDF standard encryption to protect your document.
  • Password Confirmation: Prevents typos with a confirm password field.
  • Instant Results: Get your encrypted PDF immediately.
  • No File Size Limit: Protect PDFs of any size (within browser memory limits).
  • User & Owner Password: Sets both user and owner password for full protection.
