What Is a PDF Page Counter?

A PDF Page Counter is a simple tool that tells you how many pages are in a PDF document. Whether you're working with a short 2-page contract or a lengthy 500-page report, knowing the exact page count helps you plan printing, estimate reading time, or verify document completeness. Our free online PDF Page Counter processes your file entirely in your browser, meaning your PDF is never uploaded to any server. This makes it one of the fastest and most private PDF page counting tools available online.

Why Use Our PDF Page Counter?

Most online PDF page counters require you to upload your document to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can count pages in any PDF instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees. Whether you're checking a 1-page invoice or a 1000-page manual, the result appears in a fraction of a second.
