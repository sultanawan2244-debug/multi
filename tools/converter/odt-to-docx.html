What Is ODT to DOCX Conversion?

ODT to DOCX Converter is a tool that transforms OpenDocument Text files (.odt) — the format used by LibreOffice, OpenOffice, and Apache OpenOffice — into Word documents (.docx). Our converter extracts the text content from the ODT file's XML structure and creates a properly formatted Word-compatible document. Our free online ODT to DOCX Converter processes everything directly in your browser, meaning your files are never uploaded to any server.

Why Use Our ODT to DOCX Converter?
