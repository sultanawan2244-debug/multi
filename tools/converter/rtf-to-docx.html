What Is RTF to DOCX Conversion?

RTF to DOCX Converter is a tool that transforms Rich Text Format files (.rtf) into Word documents (.docx). RTF is a document file format developed by Microsoft that supports basic formatting like bold, italic, fonts, and colors. Our converter parses the RTF commands to extract the text content and creates a properly formatted Word-compatible document. Our free online RTF to DOCX Converter processes everything directly in your browser, meaning your files are never uploaded to any server.
