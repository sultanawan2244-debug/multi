What Is SRT to VTT Converter?

SRT to VTT Converter transforms SubRip subtitle files (SRT) into WebVTT format (VTT). It converts SRT timestamp format (hours:minutes:seconds,milliseconds) to VTT format (hours:minutes:seconds.milliseconds) and adds the WEBVTT header.
