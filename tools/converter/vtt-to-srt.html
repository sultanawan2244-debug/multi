What Is VTT to SRT Converter?

VTT to SRT Converter transforms WebVTT subtitle files (VTT) into SubRip format (SRT). It strips the WEBVTT header, converts timestamp format from dots (.) to commas (,) used by SRT, and renumbers cue indices sequentially.
