What Is GIF to MP4 Converter?

GIF to MP4 Converter is a free online tool that transforms animated GIF files into WebM video format. GIFs are limited to 256 colors and produce large file sizes, while WebM video offers better compression, true color support, and more efficient playback. Our tool captures GIF frames by rendering them to a canvas and recording the output with the MediaRecorder API, all within your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Why Use Our GIF to MP4 Converter?

Converting animated GIFs to video format reduces file size significantly while improving visual quality. WebM video supports millions of colors versus GIF's 256-color limit, and video codecs provide much better compression. Our browser-based approach means your files remain private and are processed instantly with no server interaction.

Key Features of Our GIF to MP4 Converter

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing. No uploads, no servers, no data transfer.
