What Is Images to PPTX Conversion?

Images to PPTX Converter is a tool that transforms your images into a PowerPoint presentation (.pptx). Each image you upload becomes a separate slide, centered and fitted to the slide dimensions. This is perfect for creating photo albums, portfolio presentations, product showcases, and visual decks. Our free online Images to PPTX Converter processes everything directly in your browser using pptxgenjs, meaning your files are never uploaded to any server.

Why Use Our Images to PPTX Converter?

Most online image-to-PPTX converters require you to upload your images to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits, watermarks, or paid subscriptions. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using JavaScript, you can create presentations of any size instantly, without worrying about privacy, data caps, or recurring fees.
