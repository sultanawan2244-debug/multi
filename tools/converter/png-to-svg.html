What Is PNG to SVG Conversion?

PNG to SVG Converter is a tool that transforms raster PNG images into SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) format. SVG is a vector image format that can be scaled to any size without losing quality, making it ideal for logos, icons, illustrations, and web graphics. Our converter offers two modes: Embedded Image mode wraps your PNG inside an SVG container (fast and lossless), while Trace mode uses edge detection to create actual vector paths from your image (best for simple graphics with solid colors). Our free online PNG to SVG Converter processes everything directly in your browser, meaning your files are never uploaded to any server.
