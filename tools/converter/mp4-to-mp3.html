What Is MP4 to MP3 Converter?

MP4 to MP3 Converter is a free online tool that extracts the audio track from MP4 video files and converts it to MP3 format. Using the Web Audio API to decode the video's audio stream and the LAME MP3 encoder (via lamejs) for compression, our tool processes everything entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, making this one of the most private and secure audio extraction tools available.

Why Use Our MP4 to MP3 Converter?

Most online video-to-audio converters require you to upload your video to a server, submit personal information, or pay for premium features. Our tool eliminates all of these problems. Since everything runs in your browser using the Web Audio API, you can extract audio from any video file instantly, without worrying about privacy, file size limits, or hidden charges. You can process videos of any length or resolution within your browser's memory limits.

Key Features of Our MP4 to MP3 Converter

  • Browser-Based: 100% client-side processing. No uploads, no servers, no data transfer.
