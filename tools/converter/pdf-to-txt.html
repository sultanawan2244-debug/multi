What Is a PDF to TXT Converter?

A PDF to TXT Converter is a tool that extracts plain text content from PDF documents. PDF files can contain text that is not easily editable or copyable. Our free online converter uses pdf.js to extract text from every page of your PDF and outputs clean plain text, all directly in your browser without uploading files to any server.

Why Use Our PDF to TXT Converter?

Most online PDF to TXT Converters require you to upload your files to a server, wait for processing, and then download the result — which is slow, insecure, and often comes with file size limits or paid subscriptions.
