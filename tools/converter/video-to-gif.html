What Is Video to GIF Converter?

Video to GIF Converter is a free online tool that creates animated GIFs from video files. Simply upload an MP4, WebM, or AVI video, select the duration and frame rate, and our tool extracts frames and encodes them into a standard animated GIF. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and the gif.js encoder, so your files are never uploaded to any server.

Why Use Our Video to GIF Converter?

Most online video-to-GIF converters require uploading your video to a server, waiting for processing, and then downloading the result — a slow and potentially insecure process. Our tool processes everything locally in your browser. You can convert videos of any length instantly, with customizable frame rates and durations, without worrying about privacy, file size limits, or data caps.
