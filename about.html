Our Story

Ali Tools Company was founded in 2024 by Ali Raza — a passionate developer, entrepreneur, and problem-solver based in Okara, Punjab, Pakistan. Ali saw a gap in the market: small businesses and individuals were either paying hefty sums for basic software or struggling with clunky, ad-ridden free tools that compromised their privacy. He set out to build something different — a company that would deliver professional-grade digital solutions without the bloated price tags.

What began as a side project — a handful of free browser-based tools for resizing images and merging PDFs — quickly gained traction. Users loved the simplicity, speed, and the fact that nothing ever left their device. Encouraged by the response, Ali expanded both the toolset and the vision. Today, Ali Tools Company is more than just a toolkit. It is a full-service digital agency offering custom software development, POS systems, website design, and social media marketing — alongside 140+ free online tools used by millions across 50+ countries.

At the heart of everything we build is a simple belief: technology should serve people, not the other way around. Whether it is a free image compressor used by a student in Kenya, a POS system powering a retail store in Lahore, or a marketing campaign that helps a brand find its voice — our work is measured by the real-world impact it creates. Ali leads a growing team of designers, developers, and strategists who share this mission.
